What is DevOps and why is it needed?

Why it is useful to work with DevOps, where to apply the technology and what tools are available.

What is this technology

The term DevOps is derived from the English words development and operations. It is an approach, methodology and even culture and philosophy of the development process where programmers, testers and system administrators can work on the product faster and more efficiently. The approach helps to reduce errors in the handover of the project from developers to testers and sysadmins and to establish communication between them. It is based on the idea that developing, testing and operating digital products is a single, seamless and cyclical process.
